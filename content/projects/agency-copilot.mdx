A full-stack platform designed for sports agents to monitor all their players' statistics in one place, with automated daily email notifications to stay informed without manual checking.

## Features

- **Player Dashboard:** Track stats for players across NBA, WNBA, and college basketball leagues
- **Game Schedule:** Upcoming games with venue details and broadcast info
- **Daily Email Notifications:** Automated recap emails via cron jobs summarizing player performance
