An AI voice assistant that handles restaurant calls for you — make reservations and check current wait times without any manual phone interaction.

## How It Works

1. **Request** — Tell the agent which restaurant and what you need
2. **Call** — The agent places a real phone call via Twilio
3. **Converse** — AI handles the conversation naturally with staff
4. **Confirm** — Receive confirmation of your reservation or wait time

Built with Twilio, Google Gemini, FastAPI, and Next.js.
