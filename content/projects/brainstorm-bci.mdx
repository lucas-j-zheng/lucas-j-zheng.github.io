A real-time visualization tool designed to guide neurosurgeons in optimally placing brain-computer interface (BCI) arrays over the motor cortex. **2nd Place Winner** in Track 2 of the Brainstorm Precision Neuroscience BCI Hackathon 2026.

## Solution

- **Interactive Surgical UI:** Built an intuitive interface for real-time BCI array placement visualization
- **Signal Processing Pipeline:** Robust preprocessing to clean and analyze raw neural data
- **Motor Cortex Mapping:** Visualizations to identify optimal electrode positioning

Precision in BCI placement is crucial for patients with paralysis or neurological conditions who depend on these devices for communication and motor control.
