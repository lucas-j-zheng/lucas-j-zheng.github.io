## Overview

A research project investigating hierarchical reinforcement learning (HRL) through custom MiniGrid environments designed to isolate and study the challenges of Montezuma's Revenge.

## Motivation

Montezuma's Revenge is a notoriously difficult benchmark for reinforcement learning due to its sparse rewards and requirement for long-horizon planning. This project creates simplified environments with progressively increasing complexity to systematically investigate where and why skill-based (hierarchical) approaches struggle.

## Approach

- **Custom MiniGrid Environments:** Built environments that capture key challenges of Montezuma's Revenge at varying difficulty levels
- **Hardcoded Skills:** Implemented predefined skill primitives to study hierarchical policy learning in isolation
- **Complexity Analysis:** Progressive environment complexity to pinpoint failure modes in HRL methods
