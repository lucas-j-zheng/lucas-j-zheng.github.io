## Overview

A real-time visualization tool designed to guide neurosurgeons in optimally placing brain-computer interface (BCI) arrays over the motor cortex. **2nd Place Winner** in Track 2 of the Brainstorm Precision Neuroscience BCI Hackathon 2026.

## Challenge

Track 2 tasked teams with building software to help surgeons identify the optimal placement region for BCI electrode arrays—a critical decision that directly impacts patient outcomes and device efficacy.

## Solution

- **Interactive Surgical UI:** Built an intuitive interface allowing surgeons to visualize and plan BCI array placement in real-time
- **Signal Processing Pipeline:** Implemented robust preprocessing to clean and analyze raw neural data from BCI sensors
- **Motor Cortex Mapping:** Visualizations to identify optimal electrode positioning over motor regions

## Impact

Precision in BCI placement is crucial for patients with paralysis or neurological conditions who depend on these devices for communication and motor control. This tool aims to reduce surgical uncertainty and improve long-term BCI performance.
