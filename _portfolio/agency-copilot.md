## Overview

A full-stack platform designed for sports agents to monitor all their players' statistics in one place, with automated daily email notifications to stay informed without manual checking.

## Features

### Player Dashboard
Track stats for players across NBA, WNBA, and college basketball leagues. View game-by-game performance, shooting splits, and upcoming schedules at a glance.

![Dashboard](/images/dashboard_stats.png)

### Game Schedule
See when your tracked players are playing next, with venue details and broadcast information.

![Schedule](/images/calendar_stats.png)

### Daily Email Notifications
Automated daily recap emails delivered via cron jobs, summarizing how each tracked player performed.

![Email Notifications](/images/Daily_stats_email.png)

## Technical Stack

- **Frontend:** Full-stack web application
- **Data Integration:** NBA, WNBA, and college basketball stats APIs
- **Notifications:** Cron job-based daily email system
- **Deployment:** Vercel

## Why It Matters

Sports agents manage multiple clients across different leagues. Agency Copilot consolidates player monitoring into a single platform, saving time and ensuring agents never miss important performance updates.
